1. Slice onions into strips.
2. Slice carrots into strips.
3. Slice bell peppers into strips.
4. Slice Shittake mushrooms.
5. Chop chives into 2-inch lengths.
6. Add 6 cups of water and 120g of dangmyeon in a pot. Turn the heat to medium.
7. Add 1/2 tbsp of dark soy sauce to the pot.
8. Once water starts to boil, wait 6 minutes.
9. Transfer noodles to a bowl and mix in 1.5 tbsp of sesame oil.
10. In a small bowl, add 1.5 tbsp of sugar, 1.5 tbsp of soy sauce, 1 tbsp of oyster sauce.
11. Add 2 tbsp of cooking oil in a pan. Stir-fry 250g of seasoned pork, 1 tbsp of minced garlic, 1 tbsp of onions, 1 tbsp of carrots, and 1 tbsp of bell peppers.
12. Once the vegetables are slightly cooked, add 1 tbsp of Shittake mushrooms, 1 tbsp of chive, and the sauce from step 10.
13. Change the heat to low and mix in the noodles.
14. Plate the noodles on the bottom and the veggies and meat on top.
