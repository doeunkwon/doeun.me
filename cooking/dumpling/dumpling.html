DUMPLING




ground pork
garlic
ginger
Shittake mushroom
cilantro
soy sauce
black rice vinegar
sesame oil
chili oil
white pepper
five spice
dumpling wraps
eggs

1. Mince half a head of garlic.
2. Mince 2 slices of ginger.
3. Chop 1/2 cup of Shittake mushrooms into bits.
4. Chop 1/3 cup of cilantro into bits.
5. In a bowl, mix in 1 lb of ground pork, garlic, ginger, Shittake mushrooms, cilantro, 2 tbsp of mushroom water, 1 tbsp of Shaoxing or white wine, 1 tsp of soy sauce, 2 tsp of sesame oil, 1/4 tsp of white pepper, 1/4 tsp of five spice, and 1 egg.
6. Let the mix marinate for 10 to 15 minutes.
7. Whisk together 1 egg and 1 tbsp of water to make the egg wash.
8. Use the filling from step 5, the dumpling wraps, and egg wash to form the dumplings.
9. Boil water in a large pot.
10. Add the dumplings in the boiling pot. They're ready once they start to float.
11. Mince and pan fry 2 cloves of garlic.
12. In a small bowl, mix in 3 tbsp of soy sauce, 1 tbsp of plain rice vinegar, and 1 tsp of chili oil.
13. Plate the dumplings, top with the garlic from step 11, and add the sauce from step 12.
