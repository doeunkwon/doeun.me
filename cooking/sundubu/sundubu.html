1. Chop green onions.
2. Dice onion.
3. In a large bowl, mix 1 cup of chilli pepper flakes, 1 tbsp of sugar, 1 tbsp of mirin, 4 tbsp of soy sauce, 2 tbsp of oyster sauce, 2 tbsp of fish sauce, and 2 tbsp of sesame oil.
4. Add 4 tbsp of cooking oil to a large pan.
5. Stir fry 1 cup of ground pork and 1 cup of green onion.
6. Add 1 cup of diced onions to the stir-fry.
7. Add 1/3 cup of minced garlic to the stir-fry.
8. Add the seasoning from step 3 to the stir-fry.
9. Add 1/2 cup of water in a pot.
10. Add 2 tbsp of seasoning paste from step 8 to the pot and let it boil.
11. Store the remaining paste in plastic bags, each containing 2 tbsp.
12. Add the Korean soft tofu.
13. Decorate the jjigae with chopped green onions, chilli peppers, and raw egg.
