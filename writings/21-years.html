Way She Goes

So apparently I'm now 21. I still feel 16. Tomorrow, I'll be asking how I turned 40. Before then, I'd like to sum up 21 of my favourite lessons that I've learned so far. This is a framework for my philosophy on life. I don't claim to practice everything listed, but what's important to me is that I'm now aware of these things and I'm making a meaningful effort to eventually nurture these concepts into reality. Some of these may sound cliché, but I think the real value lies in how deeply one is willing to explore them.

1. Take it all in
2. Life is a marathon (pace yourself)
3. Start now
4. Hard work will somehow pay off
5. Show respect where it’s due
6. The past doesn’t buy respect
7. Stand firm on your beliefs
8. Be open to change
9. Listen
10. Assume the best in people
11. Connect the dots
12. Question authority
13. 1 truth is better than 100 lies
14. Practice forgiveness often
15. Show love while you still can
16. Life is tragic
17. Life is beautiful
18. Don't fall in too deep
19. The universe is unbiased
20. This isn't your practice life
21. Dance and have fun
