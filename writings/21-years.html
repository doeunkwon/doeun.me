21 Years

By Doeun Kwon
04/12/22

So it goes.

Here's 21 of my favourite lessons that I've learned in my 21 years alive. I don't claim to practice everything listed, but what's important to me is that I'm now aware of these things and I'm making an honest effort to eventually nurture these ideas into reality. Some of these may sound cliché, but I think the real value lies in how deeply one is willing to explore them.

1. Take it all in
2. Life is a marathon (pace yourself)
3. Start now
4. Hard work will somehow pay off
5. Show respect where it’s due
6. The past doesn’t buy respect
7. Stand firm on your beliefs
8. Be open to change
9. Listen
10. Assume the best in people
11. Connect the dots
12. Question the herd
13. 1 truth is better than 100 lies
14. Practice forgiveness often
15. Show love while you still can
16. Life is suffering
17. Life is rewarding
18. Don't fall in too deep
19. The universe doesn't care
20. This isn't your practice life
21. Let it be

Time to celebrate.

My most important realization is that I'll always be flawed. I feel like most of my negativity stems from my false belief that I can somehow reach perfection. We're all gonna mess up badly at some point in our lives. But what do you expect from a bunch of slightly-advanced breed of monkeys? Life is about learning from our mistakes, moving on, and celebrating everything in between. Forgive yourself and just strive to be better. Here are two songs that inspired me to write this:

1. The Weeknd - Less Than Zero
2. 070 Shake - Guilty Conscience
