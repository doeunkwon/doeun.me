Computer science is the science of computing - a study that goes beyond the realm of electronic computers. This field of study fascinates me, not because I like computers, but because I like to problem-solve. My favourite topics in mathematics are algorithms and combinatorics because I think counting, or computing, is useful. Through counting, we’re able to quantify, compare, and analyze many important things. But counting solves just a small chunk of all problems.

There are problems that probably can’t be solved through computation and I find that interesting. I’m curious about human interactions and experiences. Chairs, raves, plants, and movies are just a few things that interact with humans. If an interaction is a problem instance, then the experience is a proposed solution. To solve the problem is to orchestrate an experience that influences people in a way that is intended by the orchestrator. One solution that I’m interested in comes in the form of software products. I aspire to create new experiences for people using my mind and hands.
